<h2>What Is Apollo.io?</h2>
Apollo.io is a platform focused on sales intelligence and outreach. It is better suited for startups and growing sales teams in small to medium-sized businesses.

Contact databases
Email sequencing
Prospecting automation
CRM integrations
AI outreach
It is understandable why many companies opt for Apollo; it is a value platform that amalgamates both lead identification and outreach.
What Is ZoomInfo?
ZoomInfo is a high-level business-to-business (B2B) data platform that caters to enterprises with a substantial business database and sophisticated sales intelligence.

It provides:
Business contact information

Data enrichment
Advanced filters
Enterprise level integrations
